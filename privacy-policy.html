Privacy Policy — Orbit Jump

Last updated: 23 November 2025

Orbit Jump (the “Game”) is developed by AdGames Dev Team (“we”, “our”, or “us”). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Game.

1. No Data Collection

Orbit Jump does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data from users.

In particular, we do not collect:

  • Name or contact details (email, phone number, address)
  • Location data (GPS or approximate location)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Contacts, photos, media, or files
  • IP address or network information
  • Analytics or usage tracking data

2. No Internet Access

The Game does not require an internet connection and does not communicate with any remote servers or online services.

3. No Ads or Third-Party Services

Orbit Jump:

  • Does not display advertisements
  • Does not use Google AdMob or any ad network
  • Does not use analytics services (such as Firebase, Unity Analytics, etc.)
  • Does not include third-party SDKs that collect user data

4. Local Storage (On Your Device Only)

The Game stores a small amount of data locally on your device solely for gameplay purposes:

  • High score information
  • Ghost mode replay data

This data is stored only on your device, is not transmitted anywhere, and is not shared with any third party. You can remove this data at any time by uninstalling the Game from your device.
